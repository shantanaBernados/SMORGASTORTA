    Did You Know?

    • Smörgåstårta

      • (English: "sandwich-cake" or "sandwich gateau") is a Scandinavian cuisine dish that is popular in Sweden. This savoury cake is compositionally similar to a sandwich, but it has such a large amount of filling that it more resembles a layered cream cake with garnished top. Smörgåstårta is served cold and sliced like a dessert cake.
