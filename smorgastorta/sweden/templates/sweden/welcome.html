What is Smorgostorta?

Smorgostorta is an application that predicts three things.

Self-Rated Health

People nowadays are prone to sickness and diseases due to the constant pressures they receive from their surroundings. Fortunately, there is an indicator within oneself which can tell if one is getting ill. This indicator is a widely accepted health measure and is called Self-rated health (SRH). SRH can help determine whether a person is likely to get sick as it is a collation of several aspects of health, such as mental, emotional, and physical.

Sickness Absence Risk

Managing sickness absence, be it on a small scale business or a large company, is crucial, as sickness absence poses a risk to any company. With a poor risk management, the business might suffer several losses. So, there is a need to assess and predict if a person has an over-risk of sickness absence, to ensure and provide a better risk management scheme, and to safeguard the welfare of the working people.
